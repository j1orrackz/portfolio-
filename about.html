About Me

My name is Joshua Onakoya, and I am a 24-year-old university student studying at Goldsmiths, University of London. I am currently developing my skills in web development, with a focus on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy building responsive and user-friendly websites.

Alongside front-end development, I have experience creating and managing e-commerce websites using Shopify, where I have worked on layout customisation, product organisation, and overall user experience. These experiences have helped me develop a strong understanding of both technical implementation and visual design.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy watching and playing football, as well as going to the gym to maintain an active and healthy lifestyle. These interests help me stay disciplined, motivated, and balanced alongside my academic work.
